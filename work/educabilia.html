Educabilia

Learn new things everyday.

Educabilia is a latin-american startup who is giving people the power to teach what they know, and learn new exciting things everyday. I was asked to work on realigning the app interface, with special attention into making new version device friendly.

  • Date

    Aug 2012 - Nov 2012

  • State

    Not live yet

  • URL

    www.educabilia.com

Old course page presented a couple of usability problems, so we realign it highlighting courses key information (date, location, modality) while keeping a general brand aesthetic but improving in the details.

Homepage and event list pages were also updated, fixing some usability issues and refreshing the look & feel of them, to stay consistent with new design direction.

All updates were done using mobile-first methodology, so the new site fits perfectly into any device.
