Zokos

Do something amazing.

Zokos a a NY startup who aims to make it easier for people to connect in real life, over food. I joined the Zokos team to work on the user interfase and the front-end of the app.

  • Date

    Dec 2011 - Dec 2012

  • State

    Live

  • URL

    www.zokos.com

Since events were the core of the idea, event view page was, with no doubt, the most important page of the site. We did a lot of A/B testing on it, refined on the fly, and ended up with a version everybody loved. We even implemented in-place editing for event information.

We also needed a place where all the info related to events & food could be digested by the user, so we created a grid-based user dashboard.

Before going live, we worked on the idea of an effective landing page. We ended up doing a version where not everything is explained, but info & mystery are balanced in order to attract people to put their emails.

After a couple of months, an important feature was added to the app: curated ideas for parties, or Zokits. We worked on the UX for adding them to the flow and ended up a some extra (optional) steps in the event create process, that helped users to throw parties faster.
