Wulia

Let’s put our city in the startups map.

Wulia is a proyect from me and a couple of local friends, trying to connect the points in our own city startup environment. I’m doing a bit of everything here, including brand creation, website design, idea generation and event organization.

  • Date

    March 2012 - Actuality

  • State

    In transition

  • URL

    www.wulia.com.ar

After defining our mission, we started working on the brand. We wanted the logo to be something that shares same passion as our message. So I went for a superhero-like icon, using the initial letter.

We also created a quick website, showcasing speakers from each event. All our speakers talked about trending tecnology topics.

Right now the idea is shaping into a bigger thing than just events, we are doing a blog, contacting people from all around the city and planning future events in order to create a powerful local community who can change the world we live in.
