Wulia

Let’s make our city awesome.

Wulia is a proyect from me and a couple of local friends, trying to connect the dots in the startup environment of Cordoba, the city where we live. I’m doing a bit of everything here, including brand creation, website design, ideas generation and event organization.

  • Date

    Aug 2012 - Nov 2012

  • State

    Live

  • URL

    www.wulia.com.ar

After defining our mission, we started working on the brand. We wanted the logo to be something that shares same passion as our message. So I went for a superhero-like icon, using the initial letter.

We also created a small website, showcasing speakers from each event. We focused on showing our passion for startups & good simple design.

On our first event, I did a presentation about Sass & Compass, in front of a good number of designers who learnt about using this awesome tool in their own jobs. The slides of the presentation, in spanish, are available here.

Right now the idea is shaping into a bigger thing than just events, we are doing a blog, contacting people from all around the city and planning future bigger events in order to create a powerful startups community who can change the world we live in.

What tools do we use to build the front-end?

Isologotype was done in paper + Adobe Illustrator. The idea for the website was first sketched in paper, then moved to Balsamiq Mockups propotypes, and finally designed in Photoshop.

The front-end part is just a simple static site that updates the file it’s loading every time a new event gets created. We used semantic HTML5 for the markup and Sass + Compass + Susy for the visual styling.

New blog is coming soon, using the awesome Octopress.
